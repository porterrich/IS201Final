Why I love Wakesurfing:

  1. It is a great way to spend time with friends and family!
    • I have grown closer with my dad by going surfing with him every summer that I can remember.
    • It is a amazing way to relax and grow closer with your friends!

      • Here is a picture of me boating with my Friends!


  2. It satisfies my desire for self-improvement!
    • I have always loved striving for improvement and in wakesurfing there is always a way to improve.
    • Nothing feels better than landing the trick you have been working towards for a long time.

      • Here is a video of me landing a Front-Side Air!


  3. It is the most refreshing Summer Time Activity!
    • Nothing beats the refreshment of jumping into the lake on a hot summer day!
    • It even just looks refreshing!
